The next season of Dancing With The Stars is right around the corner. With the upcoming season beginning soon, the show has begun a flux of announcements related to this show’s cast.

Alfonso Ribeiro, Julianne Hough Set to Co-Host to Dancing With The Stars Season 33

On August 13, Dancing With The Stars shared a promotional video for the upcoming season of the show. The promotional video features Julianne Hough ballroom dancing with Alfonso Ribeiro. In the background are the show’s well known professional cast. Although not listed by name, Emma Slater, Sasha Farber, Daniella Karagach, Pasha Pashkov, Britt Stewart, Alan Bersten, Brandon Armstrong, Rylee Arnold can be seen in the short promo.

At this time it’s unknown whether Val Chmerkovskiy, Jenna Johnson, Artem Chigvintsev, KoKo Iwasaski, and Gleb Savchenko will return for the season, since they’re not seen in the video.

“Step into the spotlight with @TheRealAlfonsoRibeiro and @JulesHough when our #DWTS hosts waltz back to the ballroom Tuesday, Sept 17 on ABC and Disney+! Stream on Hulu,” the show wrote alongside the video.

Carrie Ann Inaba, Bruno Tonioli, Derek Hough return to Dancing With The Stars Judging Panel

At the time, the show also confirmed the upcoming seasons’ judging panel. Unsurprisingly, longtime judges Carrie Ann Inaba and Bruno Tonioli are set to return to the panel. They will once again be sat alongside professional dancer turned judge Derek Hough. This will be Hough’s fifth season on the judging panel.

“It’s a 10 from us! 🤩 We’re thrilled to have @CarrieAnnInaba, @BrunoTonioliOfficial, and @DerekHough back as our judges when #DWTS premieres Tuesday, Sept 17 on ABC and Disney+! Stream on Hulu,” the show wrote on Instagram.

Due to this announcement, it can be assumed that Len Goodman’s spot on the judging panel will once again remain unfilled in this season.

The next season of Dancing With The Stars premieres on September 17 on ABC, and Disney+. Fans will also be able to stream the show on Hulu.
