Movie starring Uru Eke, OC Ukeje, Enyinna Nwigwe gets release date

 Posted on June 7, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  171 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

According to the Producer, the movie will hit Nigeria cinemas on March 18, 2016.

Produced by Uru Eke, 'Remember Me' stars Femi Jacobs, OC Ukeje, Rich Tanksley, Enyinna Nwigwe, Victor Olaotan, Anthony Manjaro, Chigul (Chioma Omeruah) and is directed by Izu Ojukwu.
