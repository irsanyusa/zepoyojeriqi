The Masked Singer runner-up JoJo just announced tour dates for 2022, and tickets are on sale very soon. The singer posted about the upcoming tour on social media. JoJo will be performing in various cities in both North America and Europe.

JoJo Announces 2022 Tour Dates

JoJo will kick off next year’s tour on February 19 in the Canadian city of Edmonton. She’ll travel around Canada and the United States before finishing the North American leg of the tour on April 16 in Ottawa. The U.K. and European leg of the tour will start May 3 in London.

The singer shared that presale for tickets to the tour begins Tuesday in select markets, with the general sale starting Friday, October 29, at 10 am local time. You can go to JoJo’s website to buy tickets for your preferred show.

JoJo recently performed a much smaller tour, with dates in several U.S. cities during October. The tour went along with the release of her recent EP trying not to think about it, which features 12 songs including “Worst (I Assume).”

After ‘The Masked Singer,’ JoJo Feels More Confident

Last year, JoJo had to cancel tour dates due to the pandemic. The singer told Demi Lovato earlier this year that being away from the stage made her wonder if she could still perform live. Thankfully, her experience on The Masked Singer helped her regain her confidence.

“Doing the show The Masked Singer that I did earlier this year kind of gave me a confidence back of performing, because I hadn’t been in front of people for a little while,” she said on Lovato’s podcast. “It’s kind of, I feel like, if you don’t use it, you lose it. It’s not always true, but it’s like riding a bike. I had to get back in practice.”

JoJo performed as the Black Swan on Season 5 of the talent show. She finished the competition in second place.
