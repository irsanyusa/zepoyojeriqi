Taco Bell is testing chicken and biscuits as a new breakfast item (YUM)

 Posted on May 30, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  94 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The fast-food chain announced on Thursday that it would test chicken and biscuits in Indianapolis, Indiana for a limited time.

The $1.99 breakfast dish uses the Naked Chicken Chips, pairing the fried chicken with biscuit triangles. The chicken and biscuits are served alongside jalapeno honey or nacho cheese dipping sauce.

Taco Bell also announced on Thursday that it is bringing the Naked Chicken Chips back to the menu across the US. The fried chicken triangles will be sold with a side of nacho cheese as well as inside the new Crispy Chicken Quesadilla.
