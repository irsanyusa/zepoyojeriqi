It seems like it was just yesterday that Claire Crosby crashed her father’s Blind Audition on The Voice. The 8-year-old has become quite the viral singing sensation since her days singing “You’ve Got A Friend In Me” with her dad Dave Crosby. Now Claire posts her own covers of popular songs like Olivia Rodrigo’s “Drivers License.”

Claire Crosby Sings Hit Song ‘Drivers License’ In Adorable Video

On their family YouTube channel called The Crosbys, Claire sings “Drivers License” while playing the guitar. “I know I don’t have my driver’s license or anything like that. This is not the story for me like I’m only 8” Claire adorably says before singing.

The video now has close to 2 million views on YouTube. “This girl is no joke so talented and those high notes gave me chills,” one fan wrote in the comments section of the video. “That’s talent right there,” wrote another. The Crosbys now have close to 3.5 million subscribers on YouTube.

“Dude, I just watched her at 4 years old and now she is 8 she has grown so fast,” another wrote. Fans have literally been able to watch Claire grow up right before their eyes. Dave first posted videos with Claire singing on YouTube when she was 3 years old. They soon went viral and they were invited to perform on The Ellen DeGeneres Show after she saw their cover of “You’ve Got A Friend In Me.” This performance has over 15 million views on YouTube.

Dave previously auditioned for Season 13 of The Voice in 2017. Claire was there to cheer him on as Adam Levine, Miley Cyrus and Blake Shelton all turned their chairs. They all wanted to hear Claire come out to sing “You’ve Got A Friend In Me.” She came out onto the stage and sang the song which literally melted the hearts of America. Dave chose to be on Team Adam. He was eliminated from the competition during The Battles which came as a shock since he had such a memorable Blind Audition.

Claire Is On Her Way To Becoming A Star

Claire and her family continue to post awesome content on their YouTube channel. For the holidays in 2020 they posted a cover of “Hark! The Herald Angels Sing” with the entire family.

Claire has garnered a huge fanbase just from posting covers on YouTube. The Crosby family also just welcomed a new addition, Millie Blake Crosby

The family also posted a hilarious challenge video where they tried not to laugh at TikTok videos. The Crosby family is building a following on TikTok as well with over 92,000 followers.
