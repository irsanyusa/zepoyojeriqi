25 Cool AliExpress Gadgets That Should Definitely Be Must-Haves in Your Kitchen

 Posted on July 4, 2024  |  5 minutes  |  1028 words  |  Aldo Pusey

Cooking is such a pleasant activity, but not everyone went to a cooking school and can chop onions in a fraction of a second like the pros. And, thankfully, that’s why gadgets exist. Think about all the dishes you’ve always wanted to make, but thought they took too much time to prepare. Just check the list below. Maybe some of these things will help you reconsider!

Today, at Bright Side, we’ve compiled some of the most useful (and original) kitchen gadgets that you can get online to ease the stress of cooking. Don’t miss the Charlie Chaplin peeler so you can cook in style!

1. Stainless-steel finger protectors

No need to worry when chopping vegetables with these magic stainless-steel finger protectors that will block any accidental cut without damaging your skin (or nails). You can order them here.

2. Donut mold cutter

Baking and making sweets is one of the greatest pleasures of life, but it does require precision sometimes. To make your task easier when making donuts, get this mold that will help you make them in the same shape and size every time. You can order it here.

3. Charlie Chaplin shaped peeler

Style and humor are always trendy, which is why we loved the Charlie Chaplin peeler that will give your kitchen the vintage touch it has always needed, and peel your vegetables. You can order it here.

4. Portable mini-blender

If you don’t have time to go to the kitchen, then let the kitchen come to you instead with this portable mini-blender. Perfect for making fresh morning smoothies on the go. You can order it here.

5. Rotating plastic dispenser

With this rotating dispenser, there’s no need to keep 10 different cereal dispensers in the kitchen. Not only does it save space, but it also keeps cereal (flour, rice, or corn) fresh for whenever you need to use it. You can get it here.

6. Kitchen shelf hangers

7. 4-can fridge organizers

8. Spiral sausage slicer

This sausage slicer allows you to give a distinctive touch to sausages at the end of cooking. You can buy it here.

9. Reusable silicone lids

Clearly this is the fastest way to save your food without having to worry about cleaning Tupperware. Plus, it’s more ecological than plastic wrap. These versatile lids come in different sizes and can be ordered here.

10. Nonstick silicone pancake mold

The time when your pancakes were always just flat and round is over. Use this simple nonstick silicone mold to make your breakfast shine every morning. No need to wait for Valentine’s Day to order it, just go here.

11. Spiral vegetable cutter

This vegetable peeler/cutter is the ultimate plate decorating tool. It basically works like a pencil sharpener for vegetables. You can get it here.

12. Reusable snack bags shaped like mason jars

Use these original bags to keep your snacks fresh when you take them to work or on a hike. They’re nice, stylish, water-resistant, hermetic, and most importantly, they look like mason jars! Get these cool bags right here.

13. LEGO silicone cake molds

No need to feel nostalgic about the LEGO’s you had to leave at your parents’ place when you moved out. Now you can just create your own and then eat them! Chocolate cakes, gelatin, ice cubes shaped like your favorite characters... The possibilities are endless. Get them right here.

14. Leaf-shaped slotted spoon

And you thought spoons were boring... This cool slotted spoon is ideal to give a fun touch to your kitchen or your table. Make soup and pasta days more fun by ordering it here.

15. Corgi-shaped cookie cutters

Is there anything more fun than cute corgi shaped cookies? We’ll answer that for you, no there is not. Make sure your cookies are just as cute as they are delicious by adopting these pups right here.

16. Chicken-shaped herb container

Vegans also have the right to enjoy a proper chicken broth. Get this chicken-shaped herb container to season your broth and never look back. Plus, you won’t need to fish for oregano leaves in your pot anymore. Get it here.

17. Vegetable slicer

No need to be a professional chef to make your plates look as if they were served in a 5-star restaurant. Also, if you’re into zoodles and other types of vegetable pasta, this tool will save you hours of work. You can order it here.

18. Stainless-steel cheese grater

Forget about getting greasy hands every time you have to grate cheese. There is also no need to break your parmesan when topping your pasta. Use this stainless-steel cheese grater to do the work for you. It even has 2 blades so you can choose your favorite grating style. Order it here.

19. Sliding fridge storage racks

No wonder we’re back to the fridge, it’s a difficult place to keep tidy. If the can organizers were not enough, check out these sliding storage racks that will help you save space in your fridge without having to use a single tool. You can also get them in different colors. Order them here.

20. Multi-blade cutter

Adding chives to garnish your plate is no longer a “restaurant thing.” Save precious seconds when chopping an onion or aromatic herbs with this multi-blade cutter. Order it here.

21. Anti-spill pot funnel

Spilling food is for the weak now that the anti-spill pot funnel has arrived. Easily serve soups and broths directly from the pot. You can order it right here.

22. Multifunction avocado slicer

Avocado fans will rejoice with this multifunction avocado slicer than prevents you from both wasting avocado and cutting your hand in the process. You can slice, mash, and even remove the seed easily with this simple but useful tool. Order it here.

23. Total tea infuser

Use those tea leaves up to the last bit with the total tea infuser. Thanks to its clever design, you can also just use it as a regular infuser and let it dry on top of your cup. You can order it here.

24. Adjustable measuring spoon

Some say cooking is about precision. For those of us who love experimenting and keeping a log with precise measurements, this adjustable measuring spoon will help you make the most of your cooking experience. You can get it right here.
