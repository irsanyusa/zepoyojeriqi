Top ideas for wedding souvenirs in Nigeria

 Posted on August 19, 2024  |  9 minutes  |  1744 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

A wedding is a great opportunity to celebrate the beginning of the new family. Wedding souvenirs may vary from edible gifts to useful items that are used in everyday life. They don’t have to be very expensive but cute so that the guests feel happy. So, it must be something that will help them remember this special day. Below are some top ideas for wedding souvenirs in Nigeria that will fit your budget.

Top wedding souvenir ideas for Nigerian wedding guests

There might be thousands of wedding souvenir ideas, but everything depends on the couple’s taste, budget, and preferences.

No need to choose costly gifts as there are many cheap wedding souvenir ideas. To thank the guests for sharing the special day you can even give some DIY gifts. To commemorate the event, you can put a memorable engraving on the souvenirs. We have got top 16 wedding souvenir ideas in Nigeria that your guests will love!

A list of wedding souvenirs for guests

Everyone loves gifts. Nigerian newlyweds give little presents to those who take part in the wedding ceremony. Nigerian brides are very insistent on making their wedding gifts unique. There is no better way to appreciate your guests and family on your special day than to give a cute favor. The wedding favors should not be expensive but be a reminder of the event. Here is a list of the most exciting and memorable gifts.

1. Sets for spices

One of the most useful and pleasant gifts among the top ideas for wedding souvenirs in Nigeria for those your friends and family members who enjoy eating spicy food.

Salt and pepper jars are an inseparable couple that symbolizes sharpness and taste to life. It is even more interesting that they are always are together just like the couple hopes. Sounds like it is a wonderful souvenir for a wedding.

2. Succulents

A plant in a small pot looks very cute and can be a symbol of the birth of a new family. It is one of the cheapest though not the worst options at all.

Of course, weddings are expensive, but souvenirs don't have to be. If you are Nigerian bride on a budget, you will definitely look for some affordable wedding souvenirs. This way you can still give away a piece of happiness.

3. Travel mugs

Cute travel mug also can also be a great wedding souvenir. This is a useful favor that will always remind your guests of the memorable day. Travel mugs designs are numerous, and these items are usually not extremely inexpensive.

4. Hand fans

Exotic design fans originates from China, but it became hugely popular in Africa because of the hot climate. The eastern tradition of giving a fan as a wedding souvenir is very old, but it still is regarded as one of the top ideas.

Custom fans can be a symbolic present featuring a picture of the married couple on one side, and their vows, wedding song lyrics, Bible verses, names of the newlyweds engraved on the other.

It would be better to get some portable hand fans for the wedding guests to let them bring the handy souvenir wherever they want. A hand fan is a very useful souvenir that comes in handy, especially, at the summer wedding. In Nigeria, you can easily buy hand fans even in the street market.

5. A bottle of wine, bottle opener, and wine stopper

Customized Wine is one of the most wonderful wedding souvenir ideas! It comes in handy at every significant event, especially, wedding. A bottle of wine put on the wedding table will make the party more memorable. Wine is an obligatory attribute of the wedding that can also be supplemented by a bottle opener and bottle stopper. What an incredible combination.

With such a gift you will have an infinite space for creativity: the bottle can be decorated with a photo of the married couple, inscriptions about love and different romantic pictures. To separate this wedding souvenir from other bottles, decorate it with an elegant accessory using lace, beads with floral and geometrical ornaments. A bottle opener and stopper can be romantically themed as well.

6. Sweets in packages or glass jars

If you want your guests to have sweet memories of the wedding, you can focus on food-related souvenirs. Cookies, chocolate, honey and jam are perhaps the best sweet wedding souvenir ideas. You can either buy sweets, remove the top wrapper and wrap it in a new one or order customized souvenirs.

The only thing to take into account is that you might want to print some inscription on the wrapper, then you would need to find a printing house or a good colour printer. The more simple the gift looks, the more original and unique it is. The favors can be packed in small bags or jars.

7. Unique shot glasses

Shot glasses are another souvenir idea to create a handy gift for wedding guests. Glasses can be beautifully engraved with various inscriptions, wishes, wedding couple’s initials, names, the date of the wedding, etc. Such a practical present will help to solve a few problems at once: to provide the guests with glasses at the wedding party and make remember the event.

8. Jotters

A wedding jotter is another incredible idea for a wedding souvenir. You can even buy simple designed notebooks and create the customized jotters yourself. Giving small but useful presents to guests at weddings is not an unusual thing for Nigerians.

Everybody uses notebooks to jot down different things, that’s why this souvenir can be used to capture the most memorable moments of the special day.

Quick development of the fashion industry in Africa pushes couples to make wedding souvenirs as stylish as possible, and wedding jotters are no exception. Be sure to make a cover for jotter beautiful and personalized. The details to include are the names of the wedding couple or their initials and the date of the wedding.

It’s very easy and affordable to print some funny phrases, Bible verses and newlyweds’ names, photos, calendars, stickers, labels on the jotter cover. If the colour of the jotter matches the colour of the wedding, it looks very harmonious. Depending on your preferences, jotter style and design can be classy, shabby-chic, vintage, minimalistic, etc.

9. Umbrella

During the rainy season in Nigeria, everybody needs umbrellas, especially wedding guests. If you are looking for wedding souvenir ideas when it rains cats and dogs, then umbrella can be a right answer! Another advantage is that you can print the pictures of you and your husband on these umbrellas with ease. It’s a very affordable souvenir idea, and all you would need to do is just find a good printer.

10. Fridge magnet

Fridge magnet always comes in handy. This cute and affordable present is easy to carry until the wedding comes to its end. You can also engrave your names and date of the wedding on the magnets.

11. Customized mobile phone

It is definitely very expensive souvenir for wedding guests, but you appreciate your wedding guests that much and want them to feel unforgettably happy at this event, then you may consider this idea. Your guests will not forget this special day for sure.

It is important to give a gift from the bottom of your heart and the price on the price tag does not matter. The cool thing is that guests can take their own wedding photos and make videos. So, if you want your wedding to be as memorable as possible, you are going to give your guests the best wedding souvenir they ever got in their lives.

12. Sunglasses

To show your guests that you care about their comfort, you can buy a pair of sunglasses, customized with your names or just having it plain. This souvenir is really a very useful one, and your guests can use it over and over again after the wedding ceremony.

13. Key holder

This is another useful and simple wedding souvenir idea as key holder can be used by guests for a very long while. This present is easy to adjust to the bride and groom’s needs, for example, engrave their names and date of the wedding, cover it with the picture of the couple, etc.

14. Wallet

Its probably the best present for men, but ladies can also appreciate this souvenir. For female guests, you will need to choose more female souvenir variants.

15. Carrier bag

This souvenir always comes in handy like no other. All you need is to be creative and come up with some interesting design idea. The carrier bags can be in many colours, you would just need to choose the that matches your wedding colour scheme. It’s also a simple and affordable souvenir idea, especially because you can make any changes with no harm to the fabric, print your pictures and names. Most of the guests appreciate this souvenir very much.

16. Perfumes

There exist many words to describe this souvenir but they all would mean the same - wonderful. It is one of the best gift ideas for every person and occasion. Both men and women enjoy such thoughtful presents. Choose some strong scents and for men and fresh, sweet or soft fragrances for ladies.

Want to make your wedding unforgettable? To make your wedding a memorable event, you need the right souvenirs that will give your guests a lot of pleasant memories of your wedding! When preparing the wedding souvenirs for Nigerian wedding guests, you don’t have to crack your brain or pocket to get a nice gift.

Regardless of the budget, there are many wedding souvenir ideas. Hope the lists of top 16 souvenir ideas for wedding guests souvenirs will help you fit your budget.
