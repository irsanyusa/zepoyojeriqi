America’s Got Talent Season 14 winner Kodi Lee has teased his very first music video for his original song “Miracle.” The singer shared a preview of the video on his Instagram account and encouraged fans to follow his YouTube channel.

AGT Winner Kodi Lee Teases ‘Miracle’ Music Video

In August, Lee released his first original single, called “Miracle.” We’re so used to hearing Lee cover other artists’ songs that it was nice to hear something truly his own. As he sings on the track, “Every day’s a miracle / Every hour wonderful.”

Now Lee is preparing to share his very first music video to go along with the song. He posted a clip on Instagram Monday, in which he can be seen wearing a sparkly silver jacket while playing the piano. The shot cuts to a clip of him singing when he was younger.

“SURPRISE!” the caption of the preview reads. “Guess What Guys! I’m releasing my first music video ever! HECK YEAH!! Are you subscribed to my Youtube!? Go subscribe and get ready for the ‘Miracle’ music video!”

Lee didn’t share a release date for the music video, but fans can subscribe to his YouTube channel to stay updated. Fingers crossed we don’t have to wait long.

The Singer Won Season 14 of AGT

Kodi Lee auditioned for Season 14 of America’s Got Talent, inspiring the judges with his incredible voice and his story of being blind and autistic. Judge Gabrielle Union hit her Golden Buzzer for Lee, sending him straight to the live shows.

Lee continued to impress as the competition continued, and he ended up winning the entire show. Since then, Lee has returned to the show several times as a guest performer, and he’s also appeared in the AGT Las Vegas live show.

The singer shared on TikTok earlier this year that he wrote the song “Miracle” about his experience winning AGT, saying it “changed my life forever.” We can’t wait to watch the new music video to go along with it.
