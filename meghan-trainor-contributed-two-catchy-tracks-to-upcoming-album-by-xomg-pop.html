Clash of the Cover Bands judge Meghan Trainor has written two songs for XOMG Pop’s upcoming album, Party Like a Pop Star. The album is set to release on March 10, 2023, and Trainor’s contributions are expected to be among the album’s highlights.

Meghan Trainor Teams Up With XOMG POP! For Their First Album

Trainor is a well-known figure in the music industry, having created several chart-topping hits and catchy pop songs. Her previous hits such as “All About That Bass,” “Lips Are Movin’,” and “No Excuses” have all been commercial successes and have earned her numerous awards and accolades.

In a recent Instagram reel, the six-girl group are singing inside Trainor’s recording studio. The pop star then revealed in the short clip that she made two songs of the 12 featured tracks on the album. They unfortunately, did not reveal more info as to which two songs did Trainor write, so it’s anyone’s guess at the moment.

“So exciting!! I think the two songs are “Moves,” and “Look at Her Now!” A Fan commented.

Trainor’s involvement in making Party Like a Pop Star has generated a lot of excitement among fans of both the artist and the group. The album promises to be a high-energy celebration of all things pop, featuring an array of upbeat tracks that are sure to get listeners moving and also grooving.

Trainor Shares Pregnancy Update Amid Recording With XOMG POP!

Amid working together with XOMG POP! with their debut album, Trainor has been sharing updates about her pregnancy with her fans on social media. On Sunday, February 26, the pop star posted a message on Instagram, revealing that she is experiencing “crazy round ligament pain,” and is having difficulty going to the bathroom as she reaches the five-month mark.

Trainor announced her second pregnancy in January and shared her dream of having a large family, saying that she is “halfway” to achieving her goal of having four children. The 29-year-old artist, who is married to Daryl Sabara, an actor best known for his role in Spy Kids, already has a two-year-old son named Riley with him.

Despite the challenges of pregnancy, Trainor remains focused on her music career and her dream of having a large family. Trainor’s fans are eagerly anticipating the arrival of her second child and are also excited to see what she has in store for her music career in the future.
