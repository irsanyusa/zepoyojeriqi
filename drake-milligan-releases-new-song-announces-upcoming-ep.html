America’s Got Talent Season 17 finalist Drake Milligan has released a new song called “Don’t Leave Me Loving You.” It’s part of an upcoming EP the singer will be releasing next month. Milligan is also competing on AGT: Fantasy League.

Drake Milligan Releases New Song ‘Don’t Leave Me Loving You’

On Friday, Milligan released a new song called “Don’t Leave Me Loving You.” The track shows off the classic country sound that took the singer to third place on AGT, as Milligan sings, “Come leave me hurt / Come leave me mad / Come leave my heart broken in half.”

The song will appear on Jukebox Songs, a new EP that Milligan is planning to release on February 16. The singer shared a cover image for the project on social media. It shows Milligan leaning against an old jukebox while holding his guitar.

Fans can pre-order and pre-save Jukebox Songs right now. Milligan released his debut album, Dallas/Forth Worth, shortly after appearing on AGT in 2022. Since then, he also released the single “I Got a Problem” last year.

Milligan Is Competing on AGT: Fantasy League

Milligan came in third place on AGT Season 17, but he’s returning to the Got Talent stage for the new season of AGT: Fantasy League. The spin-off features past contestants from Got Talent shows around the world.

The show’s judges have each chosen a dream team featuring 10 acts. Milligan is one of the acts on Howie Mandel’s team. During last week’s premiere, Mandel’s team did remarkably well, as four of his acts moved on to the next round.
