Winona Ryder Reveals Why She Wont Get Married, Even After Being in a Relationship for 12 Years

 Posted on July 18, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  435 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

Winona Ryder’s connection with Johnny Depp remains one of her most famous relationships ever. Keanu Reeves can also be added to the list. But the Stranger Things actress has been committed to fashion designer Scott Mackinlay Hahn since 2011. She has recently shared the ultimate reasons she doesn’t want to get married, and here we are today to reveal the truth to our beloved readers.

Relationship with Johnny Depp

Winona Ryder’s career has recently experienced a resurgence, partly due to her starring role in Stranger Things. However, the actress was once one of Hollywood’s brightest young stars. She was engaged to Johnny Depp, a rising star at the time, in the late ’80s and early ’90s. Winona and Johnny Depp met at the premiere of Great Balls of Fire! in 1989 when she was 17 and he was 26.

They were together for approximately 3 years before their saddening separation left both celebrities heartbroken. Recalling those times, Winona credits an “incredible therapist” who helped her escape that rut.

Relationship with Keanu Reeves

Jordan Strauss/Invision/AP/East News

Reeves most recently provided some information on an earlier relationship with Winona Ryder. They both admire one another, and on the set of Dracula in 1992, they got along like old friends. Fans might not be aware that a particular scene in the movie is entirely true, making both performers believe they were married.

“We did a whole take of a marriage ceremony with real priests,” Reeves said in an interview. “Winona says we are [married]. Coppola says we are. So I guess we’re married...”

Relationship with Matt Damon

Gwyneth Paltrow, who at the time was dating Matt’s BFF, Ben Affleck, and was also good friends with Winona, introduced Damon and Ryder in 1997. Shortly after meeting, the 2 started dating since they got along immediately. The couple’s romance initially slipped everyone’s notice because many thought Damon was seeing his Good Will Hunting co-star Minnie Driver.

Unfortunately, the couple’s relationship didn’t last for very long, and in the spring of 2000, they decided to part ways.

Now, she’s with Scott Mackinlay Hahn.

Chris Pizzello/Invision/AP/East News

Winona Ryder and fashion designer Scott Mackinlay Hahn have been dating for almost 10 years and have kept their relationship discreet. Hahn normally keeps a low profile, but he has supported Ryder on several red-carpet occasions. But when it comes to a wedding, the couple is still unsure.

Marriage is not a top priority for her.

Hahn and Ryder have been together for over 10 years, but they may never get married since the actress doesn’t want a divorce. In an interview from 2016, she explained, “Marriage? I don’t know. I’d rather never have been married than have been divorced a few times. Not that there’s anything wrong with divorce, but I don’t think I could do it if that were a possibility.” She added, “When your parents are madly in love for 45 years, your standards are really high.”
