Sundry Foods Limited celebrates 20 years serving deliciousness in Nigeria

 Posted on June 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  364 words  |  Larita Shotwell

A Journey from Humble Beginnings to Nationwide Fame

Starting from a small bakery in Port Harcourt, Sundry Foods has grown into a food empire with over 200 outlets across 16 states in Nigeria. From the delicious meals at Kilimanjaro to the sweet treats at Nibbles Creamery, Sundry Foods has been serving up happiness one bite at a time for the past two decades.

ADVERTISEMENT

What’s their secret? It’s all about making great-tasting, healthy food accessible to everyone at affordable prices. And they’ve been doing it so well that they now serve over 10 million customers every year. With a whopping N43.6 billion in revenue last year alone, it’s clear that Nigerians can’t get enough of Sundry Foods!

A Gala Night to Remember

To celebrate this amazing journey, Sundry Foods hosted a spectacular Gala Night at the Civic Center in Lagos, Nigeria. The event was a star-studded affair with music, dance, comedy, and more. Long-serving staff were honored, and there were plenty of fun activities to keep the party going. It was a night to remember for all who attended, and a perfect way to celebrate 20 years of delicious success.

Looking to the Future

Sundry Foods isn’t stopping anytime soon. They’re on a mission to become the top food services company in Nigeria, and they’re well on their way. With plans to expand even further, they’re going to keep serving up the food you love in more places. Plus, with over 4,000 employees, they’re one of the biggest employers in Nigeria, creating jobs and opportunities wherever they go.

So, here’s to 20 years of Sundry Foods – and here’s to many more delicious years to come!
